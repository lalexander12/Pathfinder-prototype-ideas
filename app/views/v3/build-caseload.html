Why am I seeing prison or probation data that I did not select?
You may see prison or probation data that you did not select in your caseload if a nominal has previously been managed by that region, even if they are currently not located there.

You can hide this data on the ‘view caseload’ page by selecting the appropriate 'in prison' or 'community' filter.
